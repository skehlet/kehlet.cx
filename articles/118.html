Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind
Sun Mar 20th 2005, 6:52pm
A couple undergo a procedure to erase each other from their memories when their relationship turns sour, but it is only through the process of loss that they discover what they had to begin with (IMDB).

This is a powerful and visually spectacular movie. I still can't quite find the words to describe why I liked it so much; it touches something indescribable, which in a way makes sense, given that's what the movie's about.

It won the Oscar this year for best original screenplay, and Kate Winslet was nominated for best performance by an actress in a leading role. (The photos used here are (c) 2004 David Lee.)
